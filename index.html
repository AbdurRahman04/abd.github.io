About me

I am Abdur Rahman, a Master of Science student in Applied Statistics at King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM), Dhahran, Saudi Arabia. My thesis focuses on Geospatial and Machine Learning Mortality Modeling of MENA Countries.

I completed my Bachelor of Science in Statistics from The Islamia University of Bahawalpur, Pakistan, with a CGPA of 3.93/4.00. I am broadly interested in mortality forecasting, geospatial methods, and the integration of machine learning techniques in demography and public health.
